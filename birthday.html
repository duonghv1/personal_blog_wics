Surprise birthday at school

I was not expecting it at all...

The moment I saw that iconic Porto's box, I couldn't help but feel a rush of excitement and gratitude. The memories of savoring that Porto's cake will stay with me, reminding me of the sweetness that true friendships bring. Grateful for the cake, grateful for the friend, and grateful for the moments that make life a little more delicious.
